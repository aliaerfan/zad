The zād Way

Mix & Match

Every zād plate — Heritage, The Quiet Ones, Bahari — is painted in the same cream-and-gold world, so nothing ever clashes. Stack a Bahari bowl on a Heritage dinner plate, slip a Quiet Ones salad in between — each place setting its own, the whole table in harmony.

Made to last,
designed to delight.

Every zād plate begins as a sketch — hand-drawn, then translated into a hand-illustrated design printed onto premium food-grade melamine. Dishwasher safe. Microwave safe. Joy guaranteed.
